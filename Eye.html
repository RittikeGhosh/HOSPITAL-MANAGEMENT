Eye / Ophthalmology

The Department of Ophthalmology deals with the physiology, structure, anatomy and diseases related to the eyes. Ophthalmologists have the expertise to treat all kind of eye related problems ranging from routine eye examination to eye surgery and other conditions of the eye and surrounding structures. Our team at Fortis is fully equipped with state of the art diagnostic and therapeutic equipments for treating glaucoma, cataracts, corneal diseases, aesthetics, paediatric ophthalmic diseases and disorders.

Eye / Ophthalmology

The Department of Ophthalmology at Columbia Asia Hospital, provides outpatient, inpatient and ocular emergency services by a team of consultants who are highly qualified, skilled and treat a wide range of eye disorders. Most surgical procedures of the eye are performed as day care surgeries. Evaluation of the eye is done as part of routine health check-up, as well as diabetic health check programmes.

The consultants perform complex surgeries including oculoplasty, squint correction surgery and corrective surgery following orbital trauma. The department mainly specialises in anterior chamber surgery. Phacoemulsification and implantable lenses are used for cataract surgeries. Pediatric services include evaluation of retinopathy of prematurity, visual acuity and corrective surgeries.

The department at Columbia Asia Hospital, offers evaluation and management of glaucoma in all age groups. Ophthalmologists are part of the team that provides neuro-ophthalmology care along with neurologists and neurosurgeons. This team is supported by the neurophysiology lab to evaluate visual function using modalities like visual evoked potentials.

Treatments and Procedures

  • Cataract surgery
  • Treatment of glaucoma
  • Video-assisted thoracoscopic surgery (VATS)
  • Misaligned eyes or strabismus (squints) corrective surgeries
  • Pediatric ophthalmology
  • Neuro-ophthalmology
  • Orthoptic procedures like corrective lenses, strabismic, eyepatchin
  • Common ophthalmic problems
  • 24 hour ophthalmic accident and emergency services
